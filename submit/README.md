### Activities
**1. Twice a month Seminar/Tech Competition:**

This event is organized Twice a month and features guest lecturers or students who are experts in various fields relevant to our community's interests. Topics can range from technology trends to personal development strategies. The events aim to attract a diverse audience and provide valuable insights and knowledge to attendees (or) participants (In case of tech competition). We have observed consistent attendance from young professionals, With an average attendance of 60-120 people per session/Competition. This Activity has shown a positive impact on young Professionals in excelling in their skills to make them job ready. However, we could improve by diversifying the speaker pool to include underrepresented voices and conducting more structured and pre-planned seminars to overcome any unexpected issues raised during the seminar.


**2. Group Projects:**

We could organize Group projects that allow community members to come together and contribute to the betterment of personal skills. These projects could help students to apply their knowledge and build real-world applications. These Group Project event is organized half a year, and attendance varies depending on the Groups formed where each group consists of 4 members. The successful aspect of these projects lies in creating a Flexible schedule and assigning group members to a particular group according to their level of knowledge, Which means a group contains students of different knowledge. To further improve, we aim to increase outreach efforts to attract more diverse participants and provide clear instructions and a learning/project-building path with deadlines to make them complete the task in time.

**3. Skill-building Workshops:**

Our organization conducts skill-building workshops on various topics that align with our community's needs and interests. These workshops are held every quarter and cover subjects such as Artificial intelligence, deep learning, and Software development. The attendees range from young professionals seeking career development to individuals looking to enhance their skills. These workshops have been well-received, with an average attendance of 40-70 people per session. The workshops contribute to our mission by empowering community members with practical knowledge and tools which can enhance their personal and professional lives. We aim to expand the workshop topics to address a broader range of interests and incorporate more interactive activities to enhance engagement and knowledge retention.

These activities have been successful in engaging the community, providing valuable learning opportunities, and fostering a sense of connection and collaboration. However, there is always room for improvement. We recognize the need for expert educators/instructors in our events to ensure students get valuable insights from the session. We also aim to enhance participant engagement by incorporating more interactive elements and requesting feedback to continuously improve the content and format of our activities. By addressing these areas, we can further our mission of making students all-rounders in the aspect of technology and leading future generations to transform the nation.

### Mission

To keep my mission clear and accessible to new community leaders, we will focus on straightforward and well-defined communication. We will establish a mission statement that fulfills our purpose and values, making it easy for newcomers to understand and align with the community's goals. We also develop guidelines and documentation that outline the key objectives and strategies for achieving our mission, providing a roadmap for future leaders to follow. Apart from communication and documentation, the new community leaders need to have a minimum of 8 - 9 months of experience in the community so that the new community leaders know the community guidelines and mission of the community.

### Structure

We will be using the Hierarchy structure for our community starting from the President as the Highest authority of the community to the core committee. Even though the structure is a hierarchy every member has the freedom to convey their message directly to the concerned authority, making the community inclusive and transparent. Every community member has democracy to participate in decision-making and contribute their ideas to the board/authority. The hierarchy structure is a good fit for our community, this structure is decided in view of students and college rules & regulations. This structure will help future-proof our community members an opportunity to learn and also get involved in the decision-making process.

The leadership successor's selection will be based on the election process by the community members. In which the former leader (President) will select 3-4 candidates as the nominee for the president. The former president evaluates Potential nominees based on their track record of contributions, their ability to inspire and engage with others, and their alignment with the core values of the community. The selected nominee will undergo a few procedures(competitions) to evaluate their skills, like debate competition, Public speaking(where the nominee needs to give a speech for 5-10 mins in front of community members), an Interview round with the former community leaders along with the election commissioner (Each round has some eligibility criteria to fulfill and if the selected nominee fails to reach the criteria the nominee will be dropped out of president), finally voting by the community members. This process will involve input from current community leaders and members to ensure a fair and comprehensive assessment.

Handing over to new community leaders, we will prioritize a smooth transition that minimizes disruption. We will establish a leadership succession plan well in advance, identifying potential successors and providing them with opportunities for growth and development. The new community members will be given some counseling before officially joining as the community leaders and providing all the important community documents to refer to before joining so as to know the insides of the community, we can ensure a seamless handover process that maintains the speed and progress of the community.
